Abstract

STOCKSAVVY" is a dynamic online platform dedicated to democratizing financial education by offering comprehensive lessons on stock market fundamentals. With an intuitive interface and user-friendly lessons, it equips beginners and enthusiasts alike with essential knowledge on investment strategies, risk management, and fundamental analysis. The highlight is its integrated paper trading feature, allowing users to practice trading with virtual funds, fostering confidence and informed decision-making in a risk-free environment. This initiative aims to bridge the gap between financial literacy and practical experience, empowering individuals to navigate the stock market responsibly and make informed investment choices.This project falls into the category of financial education and investment simulation. It combines online learning about stock market fundamentals with a simulated trading platform to offer users practical experience without the risk of real financial loss.
